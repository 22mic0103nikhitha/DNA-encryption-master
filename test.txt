Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), located in Vellore, 
Tamil Nadu, is one of India's most prominent private deemed universities. 
Renowned for its engineering and technology programs, it consistently achieves high 
rankings in national frameworks like NIRF and international rankings such as QS.


The university is perhaps most famous for its entrance examination, 
the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE), which attracts lakhs of 
aspiring students from across India and abroad each year.

The campus itself is a massive, self-contained ecosystem spanning over 370 acres. 
It features modern academic buildings, advanced research labs, a large central library, 
and extensive residential facilities for students and staff. One of the unique academic
 structures at VIT is the Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS), which allows students a 
 significant degree of choice in selecting their courses, time slots, and even faculty 
 members for their term.



Student life at VIT is highly diverse, with a population drawn from every state in India 
and over 50 countries. The university is also known for its large-scale 
annual fests: Riviera, a vibrant international cultural and sports festival, and Gravitas,
 a major technical and design carnival.
